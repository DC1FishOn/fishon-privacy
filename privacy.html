Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2025

FishOn ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

When you use FishOn, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Location Data: We collect your device’s location to show catches on the map and allow you to add new ones near you.
  • Photos: You may choose to attach photos of your catches. These images are stored only on your device and are not uploaded to any cloud service.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device data such as model and operating system version for performance and diagnostics.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to enhance your experience within the FishOn app. For example:

  • Displaying your catches on the map
  • Filtering results based on time or species

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing or commercial purposes. All information you provide—such as your catch records and location data—is stored securely and anonymously, and is used solely to support the core functionality of the FishOn app. Your data is not visible to other users and is not linked to your identity. Optional sign-in or authentication features may be added in future versions, but are not currently available. Future premium features may offer access to aggregated or global catch data, without exposing any personal or identifiable information.

4. Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS and securely stored in Firebase. Only authenticated users can access their own data.

5. Children’s Privacy

FishOn is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Choices

You may delete your locally stored catches at any time by uninstalling the app.
